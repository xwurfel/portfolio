## Overview

Played an integral role in developing and improving a globally recognized sports video streaming platform, working across both mobile and TV form factors to deliver a seamless viewing experience to tens of millions of users.

## Key Contributions

- Developed new features from inception through to deployment across Android Mobile and Android TV applications
- Achieved 30%+ improvement in content loading times through targeted performance optimizations
- Improved internationalization coverage by over 60%, enabling a better experience for users across global markets
- Conducted major refactoring initiatives to improve code maintainability and reduce technical debt
- Implemented robust video playback features and streaming optimizations for varying network conditions
- Worked within a large-scale multi-module codebase following Clean Architecture principles

## Technologies

Kotlin, Android SDK, Android TV (Leanback), ExoPlayer, MVVM, Dagger 2/Hilt, Retrofit, OkHttp, Coroutines & Flow, CI/CD
